Frequently Asked Questions

Membership is open to all NTOU graduates. Simply fill out our online registration form or contact us directly. There are no membership fees, and you'll gain access to our directory, events, and networking opportunities.

You can update your information by logging into your alumni account on our website, or by sending us an email with your updated details. We encourage all alumni to keep their information current.

Yes! We have various volunteer opportunities including event organization, mentorship programs, and committee positions. Contact us to learn about current openings and how you can contribute to our community.

There are several ways to support current students: join our mentorship program, contribute to scholarship funds, offer internships, or participate in career development workshops. Every form of support makes a difference.

We organize events throughout the year including our annual reunion, quarterly networking sessions, professional development workshops, and social gatherings. Check our events page for the latest schedule.
